The Future of Sustainable Technology: What to Expect in 2025

By Alex Richards â€¢ Oct 24, 2024

Sustainable technology is no longer a niche market. As global energy demands shift, the tech industry is pivoting toward solutions that offer both high performance and low environmental impact.

1. Green Energy Computing

Data centers currently consume about 1% of global electricity. In the coming years, we expect to see a surge in liquid cooling technologies and AI-optimized energy management systems that reduce waste by up to 40%.

2. Circular Hardware Economy

Major manufacturers are starting to prioritize modular designs. Imagine a laptop where you can replace only the processor instead of the whole machine. This reduces e-waste and increases the lifecycle of consumer electronics.

Research suggests that by 2030, circular economic models could provide a $4.5 trillion economic opportunity globally. This isn't just good for the planet; it's good business.
